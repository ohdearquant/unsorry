# euclid-perfect-numbers

Euclid's theorem on perfect numbers: if $2^p - 1$ is prime (a Mersenne prime), then $2^{p-1} \cdot (2^p - 1)$ is perfect.

- **Source:** Euclid, Elements IX.36
