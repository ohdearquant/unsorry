# gbinom-consec-four-fact-dvd

The product of 4 consecutive integers n*(n+1)*...*(n+3) is always divisible by 4! = 24, for every integer n. Equivalently, the binomial coefficient C(n+3,4) is always an integer.

- **Source:** self-seeded polynomial-divisibility identity family.
