# gbinom-consec-seven-fact-dvd

The product of 7 consecutive integers n*(n+1)*...*(n+6) is always divisible by 7! = 5040, for every integer n. Equivalently, the binomial coefficient C(n+6,7) is always an integer.

- **Source:** self-seeded polynomial-divisibility identity family.
