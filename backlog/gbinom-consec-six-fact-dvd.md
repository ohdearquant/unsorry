# gbinom-consec-six-fact-dvd

The product of 6 consecutive integers n*(n+1)*...*(n+5) is always divisible by 6! = 720, for every integer n. Equivalently, the binomial coefficient C(n+5,6) is always an integer.

- **Source:** self-seeded polynomial-divisibility identity family.
