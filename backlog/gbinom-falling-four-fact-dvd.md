# gbinom-falling-four-fact-dvd

The falling factorial n*(n-1)*...*(n-3) is always divisible by 4! = 24, for every integer n. This is the classical C(n,4) = n!/(n-4)!/4! integrality identity.

- **Source:** self-seeded polynomial-divisibility identity family.
