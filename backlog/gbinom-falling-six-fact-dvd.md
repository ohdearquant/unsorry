# gbinom-falling-six-fact-dvd

The falling factorial n*(n-1)*...*(n-5) is always divisible by 6! = 720, for every integer n. This is the classical C(n,6) = n!/(n-6)!/6! integrality identity.

- **Source:** self-seeded polynomial-divisibility identity family.
