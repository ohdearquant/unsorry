Added ADR-080 (Draft) — Platform Generalisation and the Self-Verification Gating Invariant (renumbered from a provisional 079 to deconflict with Leo's ADR-079). Anchored in the founding plan: the swarm is a general engine for any domain carrying a cheap, deterministic, kernel-grade self-verifier (math, formal software/hardware verification like Lion, exact-certificate constructions); domains without one (founding ranks 4–9) are out of the trustless commons so it cannot be poisoned. Composes with Leo's ADR-078 as a two-level gate: ADR-080 admits the domain, ADR-078 registers the target within it. Records the commons-not-contractor governance principle. Draft for founder ratification.
