Added a **Sourcing** view to the leaderboard page ([ADR-060](docs/adrs/ADR-060-Contributor-Goal-Sourcing-Skill.md)): a fourth toggle that surfaces the sourcing leaderboard (`docs/metrics/sourcing-leaderboard.json`) in the browser — who **sourced** each goal, ranked by goals sourced with a proved/open/difficulty split. The data was already generated by `python3 -m tools.leaderboard --sourcing` but had no browser surface; it is now one click from the contributor leaderboard, lazily fetched the first time the tab is opened.
