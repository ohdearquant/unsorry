Changelog entries are now **per-PR fragments** under `changelog.d/` instead of direct edits to `CHANGELOG.md`'s `[Unreleased]` section (ADR-040/SPEC-040-A). Every change adds one uniquely-named `changelog.d/<category>-<slug>.md` file, so concurrent PRs never conflict on the changelog at any merge rate — the `merge=union` driver only worked on local rebases, not GitHub's conflict detection. `python3 -m tools.changelog --preview` renders the `[Unreleased]` body; `--release <version> <date>` folds the fragments into a versioned section and clears the directory (the only writer of `CHANGELOG.md`, run once per release).
