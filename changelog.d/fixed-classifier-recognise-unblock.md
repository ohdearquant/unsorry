The PR title classifier (`tools/repo/pr_labels.py`) now recognises the `unblock(<goal>):` swarm shape ‚Äî the ADR-009 step that re-opens a `blocked` parent goal once its sub-lemmas are proved. Previously this sanctioned-and-frequently-used shape (41 PRs in history) matched no known shape, so the `pr-conventions` gate falsely failed every `unblock` PR and none received a label. It now classifies to a new `swarm:unblock` label and passes the convention gate.
