Fixed Gate A failing on fork PRs because of a cosmetic comment step. The axiom audit's "Sticky footprint comment" step posts the axiom report to the PR via `gh api`, but a fork PR's `GITHUB_TOKEN` is read-only on the base repo, so the POST 403s; with `set -euo pipefail` and no `continue-on-error`, that failed the whole `gate-a-audit` job — and thus `gate-a` — even though the authoritative audit had already passed (observed on Rauxon/fork prove PRs like #3233, #3239). The step is now `continue-on-error: true`: the informational comment may fail without blocking the soundness gate. No change to what the audit checks.
