Fixed the proof graph wrongly listing the GitHub user who *merged* a proof as its **solver**. For the ~300 (mostly archived) goals with no recorded `solver≜`, the visualiser had been falling back to the squash-merge author — i.e. the dispatcher who landed the PR, not the person who solved it. The Solver column now shows the recorded AISP solver only (“—” when there is none, per ADR-023's never-guess rule), and a new **Merged by** column reports who landed the PR, so the two are never conflated.
